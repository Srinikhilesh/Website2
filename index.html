Aeromodelling:

Aeromodelling the art of building and flying aircraft models (RC Aircraft), Gliders, Ornithopters, Boomerangs and Paper Planes. Although Aeromodelling looks like a lot of Aerospace/ Aeronautical engineering topic, it involves a lot of interdisciplinary concepts from from various streams of engineering - primarily Aerospace/ Aeronautical, Mechanical, Electronics, Electrical and Computer Science. Aeromodelling gives a good understanding of the roles each of these engineering skills play in real aircraft industry and provides enormous opportunities to develop innovative thinking and implementation.

International Airshow 2015

This event was held as part of Milan, a national level cultural fest held by SRM University. 20 top radio control pilots from all over India flew their aircrafts, helicopters and multicopters. The show got even better when “Gunther Gold” was showcased live. Gunther is one of the world leading radio control helicopter pilot from Germany. The event ended on a light note with an explicit show of fireworks in the night but not before flying the night aircraft/helicopter.
